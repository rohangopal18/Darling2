About Darling

Darling (Document Aggregation & Reorganization Library for Integrated Next-Gen Generation) is a simple, privacy-friendly PDF merging tool that works entirely in your browser. Developed by Rohan Gopal, Arnold A & R Shane for easy document management.

We built Darling to make document handling fast, clean, and stress-free — no uploads, no tracking, no sign-ups.

Why Darling?

  • ✔ Runs completely in your browser
  • ✔ No file uploads to any server
  • ✔ Free and easy to use
  • ✔ Built for students, professionals, and creators
